Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our customer support line.

How can I get technical support for a course?

For technical issues with courses, please use the "Technical Support" option in the contact form or email support@academy.com with details about the problem you're experiencing.

Do you offer phone support?

Yes, our phone support is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. For weekend support, please use our email system.

How can I report a bug or issue with the website?

To report website issues, please select "Technical Support" in the contact form and provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots if available.
