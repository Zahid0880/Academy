Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need to become an instructor?

You don't need formal teaching qualifications, but you should have expertise and practical experience in your subject area. Passion for teaching and clear communication skills are essential.

How much can I earn as an instructor?

Earnings vary based on course pricing, enrollment numbers, and promotion efforts. Instructors earn a revenue share of 50-70% for each enrollment, with top instructors earning six-figure incomes annually.

What equipment do I need to create courses?

You'll need a computer, a good quality microphone, and a webcam or screen recording software. Depending on your course topic, you might need additional equipment specific to your field.

How long does it take to create a course?

Course creation time varies widely depending on the complexity and length of your course. Most instructors spend 1-3 months planning, recording, and editing their first course.

How does Academy support instructors?

We provide comprehensive resources including course creation guides, instructor forums, marketing tools, and dedicated support staff to help you succeed at every stage of your teaching journey.
