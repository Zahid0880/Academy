About Academy

Transforming lives through accessible, high-quality education

Founded in 2015, Academy has grown from a small startup to a global learning platform with over 15 million students worldwide. Our mission is to create new possibilities for people everywhere by connecting them to the world's best education.

Our Story

Academy began with a simple idea: to make quality education accessible to everyone, regardless of geographic or economic barriers. Our founders, experienced educators and technology experts, recognized the potential of online learning to democratize education.

Starting with just 10 courses in technology and business, we've expanded to offer over 28,000 courses across dozens of categories. Today, we connect millions of students with thousands of expert instructors from around the world.

Our platform has helped people learn new skills, advance their careers, and pursue their passions. We're proud of the impact we've made, but we're just getting started.
